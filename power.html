Power (パワー Pawā) is the tritagonist of the Public Safety Saga (Chainsaw Man). She is the Blood Fiend (血ちの魔ま人じん Chi no Majin) who embodies the fear of blood and a Public Safety Devil Hunter working under Makima's special squad.
